ABOUT ME

My enthusiasm for problem solving and my empathetic approach in life has led me to the world of user experience design.
My designs range from planning and synthesizing research efforts to design conceptualization, usability testing, ideation, wireframing and prototyping. I love that UX design allows me to apply skill such as curiosity, design thinking and creativity. What excites me is being able to execute a project from start to finish as a team player while creating user centered design solutions.
Using my colourful work experience, I combine a people-first mentality in all of my designs. I am searching for opportunities to demonstrate my passion for human centered design. I thrive in fast paced environments, where feedback and teamwork is encouraged.
Beside design, I love to explore new hiking trails, going for bike rides along the lake and listening to a well curated playlist.
